Geldium Finance — Delinquency Risk EDA Dashboard
A data analytics project completed as part of the Tata iQ Job Simulation on Forage.
What I did
Analysed 500 customer records to identify delinquency risk patterns using Excel and Power BI.
What I found

16% of customers are delinquent — above healthy industry levels
Unemployed customers have the highest risk at 19%
Income has the most missing data at 7.8%
Credit Utilization exceeded 100% — data quality anomaly flagged

Tools used
Excel — Pivot Tables, COUNTIF, COUNTBLANK, Conditional Formatting
Power BI — KPI Cards, Bar Charts, DAX Measures
